What it is

The SIGNAL Assessment is Velyntic's structured entry point for any business considering AI adoption. It is a focused, methodical review of your operations, your team, and your current technology that produces a clear and honest picture of where you are and where AI can genuinely help.

SIGNAL is not a sales process dressed up as a report. You will receive a governed roadmap with real recommendations, real priorities, and real honesty about what is not worth doing right now.

What the assessment covers

A discovery session with your key stakeholders, typically one to two hours
Operations and workflow mapping across your core business functions
AI readiness scoring across five dimensions: people, process, data, governance, and technology
A review of your sector's current AI landscape and what competitors are doing
Identification of three to five priority use cases with effort and value estimates
A full SIGNAL Report: your governed adoption roadmap in plain language
A post-delivery review call to walk through the findings and answer questions

Who it is for

Any SME that is thinking seriously about AI but is not sure where to start, what is realistic, or how to do it responsibly. It is also valuable for businesses that have already experimented with AI tools but want a structured view of what to do next.
