Our story

Built on delivery. Focused on what works.

Velyntic was founded by someone who spent fifteen years leading teams through situations where precision, governance, and human judgment were not optional extras. Five of those years were in special operations, planning and executing complex programmes across multiple countries, often with incomplete information and high stakes.

That background does not make us better at AI than anyone else. But it does mean we approach every engagement with a level of rigour that most AI consultancies simply have not built. We plan before we act. We govern before we scale. We document so the knowledge stays with you when we are done.

Velyntic focuses exclusively on UK SMEs because that is where the gap is largest. The big consultancies are not interested in your twenty-person business, and the freelancers you find online do not have the delivery framework to see a transformation through from assessment to sustained adoption. We built Velyntic to sit in that space.

We do not chase volume. Every client we take on is a client we believe we can genuinely help. If SIGNAL tells us AI is not the right priority for your business right now, we will tell you that too.

What we stand for

Four values. No exceptions.

These are not aspirations written on a wall. They are the criteria we use when deciding whether to take on a project, how to approach a problem, and whether something we have built is actually good enough.

01

People First

Technology serves your team, not the other way around. Every recommendation starts with your people: how they work now, what would genuinely make their work better, and what would create friction or resistance. AI that your team does not trust or use is not AI adoption. It is wasted money.

02

Governed by Default

Responsible AI is not a box to tick at the end. Oversight, transparency, and ethical guard-rails are built into every engagement from the first conversation. We help you understand what your AI is doing, why it is doing it, and how to maintain human judgment at the points where it matters most.

03

Delivered and Evolved

We do not drop a solution and disappear. Delivery is only the middle of the process. Embedding AI into how your team actually works, then monitoring and improving it over time, is where the real value is created. ADDEE exists because most AI engagements fall over after the build.

04

Built for Longevity

No lock-in, no proprietary platforms that only we can maintain, no bloated solutions that need a team to manage. Everything we build is designed to grow with your business, remain in your hands, and be maintainable by you or anyone else you choose to work with in future.

Our framework

ADDEE: Assess, Design, Deliver, Embed, Evolve

ADDEE is the delivery framework that sits beneath every Velyntic engagement. It is not a project management methodology for its own sake. It exists because AI adoption fails at predictable points, and ADDEE is designed to address each one.

Most AI projects fail because they skip Embed: the solution gets built but the team never really adopts it. Or they skip Evolve: the solution launches and then slowly becomes irrelevant as the business and the technology move on. ADDEE makes both of those stages non-negotiable.

The loop at the end is the most important part. Evolve feeds back into Assess, which means every Velyntic engagement is designed to become an ongoing partnership rather than a one-time transaction. This is how we protect your investment over time.

A

Assess

Branded as SIGNAL

Before any technology decision is made, we need to understand your business: how it operates, where the friction is, what data you have, and what your team is capable of and willing to adopt. The SIGNAL Assessment is our structured approach to this stage. The output is a clear, honest picture of your readiness and a prioritised roadmap for what to do next.

D

Design

With a clear picture of your situation, we architect the right solution. This means selecting the right tools, designing the right automations, and building in the right governance from the start. The design phase is collaborative: your team is involved because the solution needs to fit how you actually work, not how a consultant thinks you work.

D

Deliver

We build and implement with precision. Delivery is not just about speed, though we move fast. It is about quality, documentation, and making sure that what gets built matches what was designed and what your business actually needs. Every delivery milestone is agreed before work begins.

E

Embed

This is the stage most AI projects skip, and it is why most AI projects fail. Embed means training your people, documenting your systems, and making sure AI is genuinely part of how your team works day to day. Not a tool that lives in a corner that one person uses occasionally, but something that is integrated into your real workflows.

E

Evolve

AI is not a set-and-forget investment. The tools change. Your business grows. New use cases emerge. Evolve is the ongoing monitoring, optimisation, and iteration phase that keeps your AI relevant and working harder over time.

↻

Evolve loops back into Assess. Every quarter, we run a lighter version of the SIGNAL review to reset priorities, identify new opportunities, and make sure the roadmap still reflects where your business is going. This is the commercial engine that makes the Evolve Partnership worth having.
